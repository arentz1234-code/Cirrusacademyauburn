When to Use CAPS

  • Loss of control / spatial disorientation
  • Mid-air collision
  • Structural failure
  • Engine failure with no suitable landing area
  • Pilot incapacitation
  • Any situation where a safe landing is in doubt

Why Train With CAPS

  • Ultimate safety net for student pilots
